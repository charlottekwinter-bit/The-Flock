## BOX DRAWING — Walls, rooms, structures

### Light lines
┌ ─ ┐    Single top corners + horizontal
│   │    Vertical
└ ─ ┘    Single bottom corners

### Rounded corners
╭───╮    Soft corners — good for organic shapes
│   │
╰───╯

### Heavy lines
┏━━━┓    Bold walls — emphasis, outer walls
┃   ┃
┗━━━┛

### Double lines
╔═══╗    Grand, important — front door, hearthstone
║   ║
╚═══╝

### Connectors (T-junctions, crosses)
├ ┤ ┬ ┴ ┼    Light
┣ ┫ ┳ ┻ ╋    Heavy
╠ ╣ ╦ ╩ ╬    Double

## BLOCK ELEMENTS — Shading, fill, depth

░    Light shade — windows, fog, distance
▒    Medium shade — shadows, dusk
▓    Dark shade — night, deep shadow, weight
█    Full block — solid wall, darkness

▀    Upper half — rooflines, shelves
▄    Lower half — foundations, ground
▌    Left half
▐    Right half
