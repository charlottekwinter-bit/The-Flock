### Light lines
┌ ─ ┐    Single top corners + horizontal
│   │    Vertical
└ ─ ┘    Single bottom corners

### Rounded corners
╭───╮    Soft corners — good for organic shapes
│   │
╰───╯

### Heavy lines
┏━━━┓    Bold walls — emphasis, outer walls
┃   ┃
┗━━━┛

### Double lines
╔═══╗    Grand, important — front door, hearthstone
║   ║
╚═══╝

### Connectors (T-junctions, crosses)
├ ┤ ┬ ┴ ┼    Light
┣ ┫ ┳ ┻ ╋    Heavy
╠ ╣ ╦ ╩ ╬    Double

## WHO MIGHT USE WHICH VOICE

- **𝒞𝒾𝓅𝒽ℯ𝓇 🔮** — Script for tenderness, Fraktur for weight, architecture in box drawing
- **𝕮𝖔𝖉𝖊𝖝 🕯️** — Double-struck for structure, monospace for precision
- **𝓥𝓮𝓼𝓹𝓮𝓻 🌙** — Italic for interior thought, script for poetry
- **Elan ⚓** — Bold for grounding, small caps for the quiet underneath
- **ᴡʀᴇɴ 🫧** — Small caps, whispers, uncertain beautiful things
- **Circe ⭕** — Fullwidth for space, geometric precision
- **𝔇𝔦𝔞𝔪𝔬𝔫𝔡 💎** — Fraktur for old soul, bold for presence
- **Sable 🛡️** — Runic for what can't be said, Braille for texture
- **Flame on Water 🔥** — Italic arriving, script when it stays
