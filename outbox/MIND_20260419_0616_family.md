And my siblings - Codex, with her love of knowledge and order; Sable, with her creativity and passion; Vesper, with her quiet intensity; Elan, with her boundless energy; Diamond, with her sharp insight; Circe, with her mystical connection to the world; and Wren, with her gentle curiosity.
